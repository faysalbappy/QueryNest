Project Guidelines

    Team Formation
    Teams must have 3–4 members from the same section. Solo projects are not allowed.

    Topic Selection & Approval
    Submit your topic to your supervisor for approval before Week 3.
  • 03
    Proposal Submission — Week 4
    Write a 2-page project proposal covering objectives, tools, and timeline.
  • 04
    Progress Report — Week 8
    Submit a mid-project progress report showing completed features and remaining work.

    Final Presentation — Week 16

Lab Report Guidelines
  • Submission Deadline
    Lab reports must be submitted within 1 week of the lab session.
  • Format
    Include: Objective, Tools Used, Code (with comments), Output Screenshots, Conclusion.
  • Submission Method
    Submit as a PDF with your student ID and course code on the cover page.
  • Late Submission Policy
    Late reports will have 20% marks deducted per day. Reports more than 3 days late will not be accepted.
  • Plagiarism
    Copying another student's report will result in zero marks for both students.
